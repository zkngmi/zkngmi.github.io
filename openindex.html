Create and sell your precious wisdom

Set up your wallet

Once you’ve set up your wallet of choice, connect it to OpenTruth by clicking the wallet icon in the top right corner. Learn about the wallets we support.

Create your collection of Truth

Click My Truth Collections and set up your truth collection. Add social links, a description, profile & banner images, and set a secondary sales fee.

Add your truth

Upload your turthy work (image, video, audio, or 3D art), add a title and description, and customize your truth with properties, stats, and unlockable content.

List your truth for sale

Choose between auctions, fixed-price listings, and declining-price listings. You choose how you want to sell your truth, and we help you sell them!
